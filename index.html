About Noons

We gave chocolate a makeover.
Instead of using sugar or weird sweeteners, we let cacao speak for itself by sweetening itself. Instead of discarding the cacao fruit pulp, we reincorporate it in the mix with all its glorious magnesium, vitamins, and antioxidants. We add a few simple fruits and superfoods and that’s it.

Sustainability

We source cacao from small family farms in Costa Rica. They use regenerative agriculture to grow award-winning cacao. We’re not saying this will save the planet, but we were purposeful in who we source from and how it is grown. Fair and just economies don’t happen without real partners willing to be real.

Packaging

We’re tired of single-use plastic. Our box is recyclable and inner-bag is compostable. We paid to make that happen. It matters to us. We hope it matters to you.
